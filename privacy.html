Privacy Policy

Nyota Fund Guide is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect minimal data necessary to provide our services.

Data Collection

We collect and process the following types of information:

  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics about how you use the app (features accessed, time spent)
  • Device Information: Basic device type and browser information for compatibility
  • Local Storage: Your saved estimates and preferences are stored locally on your device only
  • Ad Interaction Data: Information related to ad views (managed by ad networks)

What We Don't Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
  • Financial information or bank details
  • Government ID numbers or personal documents
  • Location data (unless you voluntarily share it in calculator)
  • Contact lists or social media information

Advertising

This app displays advertisements through:

  • Google AdMob: For mobile advertising
  • Median: For rewarded and interstitial ads
  • Native Ads: Integrated content-style advertisements

Ad networks may collect:

  • Device identifiers for personalized advertising
  • IP addresses for geographic targeting
  • Interaction data with advertisements

You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings.

Cookies & Local Storage

We use:

  • Local Storage: To save your calculator estimates and preferences
  • Session Storage: For temporary app functionality
  • Third-party Cookies: By ad networks for advertising purposes

You can clear local storage through your browser settings at any time.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Limited data may be shared with:

  • Advertising Partners: For serving relevant ads
  • Analytics Services: To improve app performance
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law
