How to Apply for Nyota Fund

Complete Step-by-Step Guide & USSD Process

1 Confirm Your Eligibility

Before applying, ensure you meet ALL basic requirements:

  • Kenyan citizen aged 18-35 (Youth) OR any woman above 18 years
  • Valid National ID & KRA PIN certificate
  • Registered business or viable business idea with plan
  • Business operates within Kenya
  • Two guarantors with valid Kenyan IDs
  • Active bank account in your name

2 Prepare Required Documents

Have these documents ready before starting application:

Original National ID
KRA PIN Certificate
Business Registration Certificate
Business Plan (2-5 pages)
Recent Passport Photo
Bank Account Details
Guarantor IDs & Contacts
Proof of Residence
Advertisement
üì± Application Methods

3 Option A: USSD Application Process

Apply via mobile phone using USSD code:

üìû *483*57#

USSD Step-by-Step:

  1. Dial *483*57# from your Safaricom line
  2. Select "Nyota Fund Application"
  3. Enter your National ID number
  4. Choose application type (Individual/Group)
  5. Fill business details as prompted
  6. Enter guarantor information
  7. Confirm all details are correct
  8. Receive application reference number via SMS

USSD Charges: KSh 1 per session (Safaricom rates apply)

4 Option B: Online Portal Application

Apply through official government portal:

  1. Visit: funds.go.ke or nyotafund.go.ke
  2. Click "Apply Now" or "Nyota Fund Application"
  3. Create account with your ID number & phone
  4. Complete all required sections online
  5. Upload scanned documents (PDF/JPEG format)
  6. Review and submit application
  7. Download/print acknowledgement slip

Portal Benefits: Track application status online, receive email updates

5 Option C: Huduma Centre Application

Visit nearest Huduma Centre for assisted application:

  1. Locate nearest Huduma Centre
  2. Carry original documents & photocopies
  3. Request Nyota Fund application assistance
  4. Officer will help complete form
  5. Submit physical copies & get receipt
  6. Application forwarded to ministry

Operating Hours: 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM (Weekdays)
